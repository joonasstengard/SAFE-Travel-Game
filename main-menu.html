Welcome to the Travel Game, !

To progress in the game you have to complete tasks inside all of the following three phases of the game: Preparing for a SAFE travel, From home to destination SAFEly and SAFE stay at the destination. The next phase will open for access once the previous one has been completed.

You will collect points while progressing in the game and learning how to become a SAFE traveller. You may also collect souvenirs on your journey, which grant extra points. Look, there's one souvenir here! Try collecting it.
