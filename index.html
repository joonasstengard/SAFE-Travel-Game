Travel Game – Introduction

Embark on a journey to become a SAFE traveller

The game will take you on a journey through the necessary steps on becoming a SAFE traveller.

Preparing for SAFE travel

This chapter of the game will guide you through the preparation phase of a SAFE travel.

From home to destination SAFEly

This chapter will take the player on a trip to the destination country and guide you through the process.

SAFE stay at the destination

This chapter is where the player will arrive at the target destination and learn the final steps to a SAFE journey.

During the game while learning about all these topics you can also collect points and souvenirs. Can you get a better score than your friends?

Get Started

Before starting your journey, you may select an avatar and enter a nickname through the SAFE passport below. To start the game, press the "Start game" button!
