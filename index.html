Travel game – Introduction

The game will take you on a journey through the necessary steps on becoming a SAFE traveller.

Preparing for SAFE travel chapter of the game will guide you through the preparation phase of a SAFE travel.

From home to destination SAFEly chapter will take the player on a trip to the destination country and guide you through the process.

SAFE stay at the destination chapter is where the player will arrive at the target destination and learn the final steps to a SAFE journey.

During the game while learning about all these topics you can also collect points and souvenirs. Can you get a better score than your friends?

Before starting your journey, you may select an avatar and enter a nickname through the SAFE passport below. To start the game, press the "Start game" button!

For the time being, the SAFE travel game only supports PC and tablet devices.
