# SAFE Travel Game

A browser-based educational game designed for the SAFE project that teaches players how to become a SAFE traveller through an interactive journey.

## Overview

The SAFE Travel Game is an educational browser game that guides players through the essential steps of safe travel. The game is structured in three main phases, each focusing on different aspects of travel safety. Players can collect points and souvenirs while learning, making the educational experience engaging and interactive.

## Features

- **Interactive Learning**: Step-by-step guidance through safe travel practices
- **Three-Phase Journey**:
  - **Phase 1**: Preparing for a SAFE travel
  - **Phase 2**: From home to destination SAFEly
  - **Phase 3**: SAFE stay at the destination
- **Player Customization**: Select an avatar and enter a nickname through the SAFE passport
- **Points System**: Collect points while progressing through the game
- **Souvenirs Collection**: Find and collect souvenirs throughout your journey for extra points
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your completion status for each phase

## Getting Started

## How to Play

1. **Start the Game**: Open `index.html` in your browser
2. **Create Your Passport**: 
   - Select an avatar using the arrow buttons
   - Enter your nickname (max 16 characters)
   - Click "Start game!" to begin
3. **Navigate Phases**: 
   - Complete phases in order (Phase 1 → Phase 2 → Phase 3)
   - Each phase unlocks after completing the previous one
   - Complete all chapters within a phase to proceed
4. **Collect Points & Souvenirs**: 
   - Earn points by completing tasks and answering questions correctly
   - Look for souvenirs throughout your journey
   - Track your progress in the top and bottom navigation bars
5. **Check Results and Get Graded!**:
   - After completing all the chapters, an option to check your results appears at the end of Chapter 3

## Game Phases

### Phase 1: Preparing for a SAFE travel
Learn about the preparation phase of safe travel. Complete all 5 chapters to unlock Phase 2.

### Phase 2: From home to destination SAFEly
Navigate through the journey to your destination. Complete all 7 chapters to unlock Phase 3.

### Phase 3: SAFE stay at the destination
Master the final steps of a safe journey at your destination. Complete all 8 chapters to see your final score.
